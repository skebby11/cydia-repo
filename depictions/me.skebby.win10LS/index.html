Compatible with IOS 10+ and A12

Description:

Get Windows 10 lockscreen customized for you!

This widget, made for XenHTML will bring you a windows 10 inspired lockscreen, where you'll be able to customize many things:
  • Custom name and surname
  • Custom e-mail and choose to hide or display it
  • Hide or display user icon, in dark or light mode
  • Choose text color between white or black
  • Choose to show the clock
  • Choose to show the date
  • Choose to show the week day
  • Choose to show the default windows 10 background or your phone's one
